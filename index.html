Instructions:

  - Click to play the game

  - The timer in the format '00:00' displays your current game time.

  - The number on the top right is the number of flags you have remaining.

  - left click to uncover the squares.

  - If a square has a number in it, that is the amount of adjacent and diagonal mines to it.

  - Right click to place a flag on uncovered squares that may be mines.

  - Uncover all the non-mine squares to win.

  - lose if you uncover a mine
